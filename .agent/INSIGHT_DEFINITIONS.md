# Insight Definitions & Formulas

This document defines the financial metrics and insights Papyrus aims to extract and calculate.
All calculations must strictly adhere to these definitions to ensure consistency.

## 1. Profitability Ratios

### Net Profit Margin
- **Formula**: `(Net Income / Revenue) * 100`
- **Insight**: Measures how much net income is generated as a percentage of revenue.
- **Target**: > 20% (High), > 10% (Good), < 5% (Low)

### Operating Margin
- **Formula**: `(Operating Income / Revenue) * 100`
- **Insight**: Measures percentage of profit a company produces from its operations, prior to subtracting taxes and interest charges.

### Return on Equity (ROE)
- **Formula**: `(Net Income / Total Equity) * 100`
- **Insight**: Measures financial performance calculated by dividing net income by shareholders' equity.

## 2. Solvency & Liquidity

### Current Ratio
- **Formula**: `Current Assets / Current Liabilities`
- **Insight**: Measures a company's ability to pay short-term obligations or those due within one year.
- **Target**: 1.5 - 3.0 is considered healthy.

### Debt-to-Equity Ratio
- **Formula**: `Total Liabilities / Total Equity`
- **Insight**: Indicates the relative proportion of shareholder's equity and debt used to finance a company's assets.

## 3. Efficiency

### Asset Turnover
- **Formula**: `Revenue / Total Assets`
- **Insight**: Measures the efficiency of a company's use of its assets in generating sales revenue.

## 4. Valuation (Requires Market Data)

### EPS (Earnings Per Share)
- **Formula**: `(Net Income - Preferred Dividends) / Weighted Average Shares Outstanding`
- **Note**: This is usually extracted directly from the "Income Statement" rather than calculated, to match official reporting.

## 5. Free Cash Flow (FCF)

### Free Cash Flow
- **Formula**: `Operating Cash Flow - Capital Expenditures`
- **Insight**: Represents the cash a company generates after accounting for cash outflows to support operations and maintain its capital assets.
